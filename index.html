About Me

I'm Prajwal Poudel a Software Engineering graduate (MSc) who speaks fluent Python, SQL, and just enough JavaScript to accidentally break the UI in three different ways at once. I've mastered full-stack development, database management, IT support (yes, I've tried turning it off and on again), and web/app development, often while muttering, "Why does this work now?"

My professional journey has blessed me with strong problem-solving skills like figuring out why the code worked yesterday but not today and process optimization abilities that would make a spreadsheet weep with joy. I thrive in collaborative environments, mainly because someone needs to remind me to push to the right branch. But wait, there's more.

When I'm not wrestling with APIs or debugging life itself, I play guitar (loud enough to annoy the neighbors, soulful enough to keep them curious), sing (mostly in the shower, but occasionally on purpose), cook (my chicken rice has layers, like an onion, but tastier), and go on random drives because nothing says "I have deadlines" like getting lost at 90 km/h with the windows down and a questionable playlist on shuffle. I find love in everything even in merge conflicts (eventually). My mantra? "Perfection is not the key, consistency is."
